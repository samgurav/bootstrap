Aashiyana Resort

Hey, Take Time to Relax........

A resort placed on the pristine baga Beach near Goa, Aashiyana Resort is a happy place that resonates with nature lovers, those on the quest for quiet or even for people who enjoy Alibag's quaint side. The resort offers a very peaceful ambience, set in the midst of lush greenery, and a well-manicured lawn. It has 4 Delux rooms with the maximum accommodation capacity of 4 people in each and a Dormitory to occupy your gang of 8. The dormitory has bunk beds and a garden at the backyard. Each room comes with a twin-bed and premium facilities. The rooms are done up in soothing white and offer a balcony where you can just relax with the sight and sound of the ocean.
